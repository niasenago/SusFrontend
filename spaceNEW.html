Among Us

How to Play

Among Us is a multiplayer game where you and your crewmates must work together to complete tasks around a spaceship. However, beware as there are impostors among you trying to sabotage your efforts and eliminate crewmates!

Rules:

  • Crewmates must complete tasks to win.
  • Impostors must eliminate Crewmates and avoid being identified.
  • Meetings can be called to discuss and vote on the Impostor.
  • Impostors can sabotage the map to create chaos.
